Marketing on social media

6

Support

6.1. Of course, we offer to help you in our Support department if you have questions or issues with the platform. In this connection, we process all information you provide to us in your inquiry, as well as your Master Information and Profile Data to the extent relevant to the case.

6.2. Support information is stored with a secure third party who delivers our support system and is processed for the same period of time as your profile data.

7

Sharing personal data

7.1. We will share your information with third parties when necessary to provide you with our service. Third parties include the following:

· Companies in the Biznotifi Group

· Authorities or others in connection with legal requirements and orders, or for the purpose of protecting our legitimate interests in a legal dispute

8

Our safety

8.1. In order to protect your information from accidental or illegal destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure or access in accordance with the data protection rules in Denmark and the EU, we store all personal information with our security-approved data processors.

8.2. Our data processors are located within the EU or the US at companies that have acceded to the EU US Privacy Shield Agreement, or in third countries at companies that process data based on EU Commission standard contracts.

8.3. All our employees are subject to confidentiality, and we have restricted access to personal data to the utmost necessary.

9

Closing your Profile

9.1. You have the opportunity at any time to request that your Profile be closed. You can do so by writing to us at support@biznotifi.com.

9.2. Your Master Information and Profile Data are stored for up to 3 months after you choose to close your Profile.

9.3. If you have pending transactions on the platform or unresolved support cases, they are calculated 3 months from the end of the last open support case.

9.4. Notwithstanding the above, your Profile will not be deleted if they are included in investigations of unauthorized actions on the Platform.

9.5. Please note that your name and the content of your ad comments and private messages will not be removed.

10

Your rights

11.1. You can always see your information on your Profile, including updating your information if you have changed your phone number, mail or similar. As a user, you are responsible for ensuring that the information contained in your Profile is fully accurate and valid at all times. You can update your information through the My Profile / Settings menu.

10.2. You are also welcome to contact us for further insights into the information we process about you, as well as to update, correct, delete or disclose information that you cannot manage on your Profile yourself. All requests for this must be made in writing by email to privacy@biznotifi.com.

10.3. Please note that you cannot exercise your rights in cases where our or others' legitimate interests in continued processing exceed your interests in protecting your personal data. For example, we always reserve the right to recover debt or investigate fraud.

10.4. If you wish to complain about our processing of your data, please contact us directly. If we are unable to help you, you can take your complaint to the Data Inspectorate.

11

Changes

11.1. We reserve the right to change the Privacy Policy. If there are any significant changes, we will notify you via email or directly on the platform.
